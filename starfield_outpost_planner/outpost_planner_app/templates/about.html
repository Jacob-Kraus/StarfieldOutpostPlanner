ABOUT

Welcome to the home page!

This website is intended as a tool to help Starfield players plan out how many of which resources they will require to construct the outpost modules they want. I made this tool because it was frustrated at always missing something when I was trying to build a new outpost, and manually looking up the resource cost on the wiki or was too much of a chore. Hopefully this helps others!

(Yes, I know in-game console commands exist, but I strive to not use them.)

HOW TO USE:

The list of modules you select should update the URL, so in order to save or share your outpost module list, just bookmark or otherwise save the URL.

No account creation required for a tool this simple.
